Instruction of how to play Simon says game

Engage in the online version of Simon Says, where you press any key to kick off the game. Follow the flashing sequence of buttons, flex your memory muscles, and press the correct sequence. Successfully hitting the right buttons advances you to the next level, but beware - a wrong move ends the game. Press any key to restart and continue the challenge!
